Turning public data into decisions.

I analyze open datasets-census, health, prices, labour, demographics and turn them into clear, honest visual stories. My focus is data literacy: finding where a headline number misleads, segmenting to reveal what's really happening, and communicating the "so what" so people can act on it. Each project below is an interactive dashboard built from a real, cited source — most also come with a downloadable Tableau workbook.
